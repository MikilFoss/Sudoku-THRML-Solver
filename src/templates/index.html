How it Works

This solver uses Block Gibbs Sampling, a Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) method.

It iteratively updates blocks of cells (e.g., alternate rows or checkerboard pattern) by sampling from their conditional probability distributions, respecting the Sudoku constraints.

The Energy represents the number of constraint violations. The goal is to reach 0 energy.
